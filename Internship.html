LinkedIn Ads Case Study:

Our Turbine Leader's Digital Impact with

Targeted Video Campaigns

About the Client

Our turbine client is one of India's leading industrial steam turbine manufacturers, known globally for its innovation, engineering excellence, and reliable performance across power generation and industrial applications. With installations in over 75 countries, our turbine client is a key player in driving energy efficiency and sustainability for a wide range of industries including textiles, chemicals, and steel.

Project Objective

The aim was to enhance brand visibility and engagement among decision-makers in industrial and energy sectors through a focused LinkedIn video campaign, emphasizing our turbine client's leadership in steam turbine solutions.

Challenges

Prior to this initiative, our turbine client faced:

Limited engagement from niche industrial audiences on social platforms

Low video completion and view-through rates across past campaigns

Difficulty in capturing quality attention spans in a B2B context

Strategy and Execution

Our digital advertising team developed a precision-driven LinkedIn campaign with the following approach:

Audience Targeting:

Reached top-level professionals and engineers in energy, manufacturing, and infrastructure sectors across relevant geographies

→
Content Optimization:

Created short-format, impactful video creatives showcasing our turbine client's innovation and real-world applications

→
Engagement Strategy:

Structured campaigns to maximize video views and completions rather than direct clicks, aligning with awareness goals

Placement Control:

Optimized for in-feed delivery to ensure strong visibility and seamless playback experience

→
Performance Monitoring:

Continuously monitored VCR (Video Completion Rate) and VPI (Video Plays on Impression) to assess viewer interest and refine targeting
